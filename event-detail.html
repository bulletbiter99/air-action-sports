April 19, 2026 Airsoft

Operation Nightfall

● Delta Base — Woodland

Mission Briefing

As twilight falls over Delta Base, two factions prepare to clash in a high-stakes woodland operation. Operation Nightfall is a full-day airsoft event built around squad-based tactics and objective-driven gameplay. Expect fast rotations, flanking manoeuvres through dense treeline, and coordinated assaults on fortified positions.

Teams will be briefed on mission objectives at staging. Communication, teamwork, and smart positioning will decide the outcome. Whether you're a seasoned operator or stepping onto the field for the first time, this event is designed to deliver intense, fair, and unforgettable gameplay from first light to last round.

What's Included

  • Full-day airsoft gameplay
  • Safety briefing and equipment check
  • Trained marshals on field
  • Tea/coffee at staging area
  • Free parking
  • Gear hire available (additional cost)

Rules & Requirements

  • Minimum age 12 (under 16 with adult)
  • Full face protection mandatory
  • FPS limits strictly enforced (350 AEG / 500 bolt-action)
  • No blind firing
  • No physical contact
  • Completed waiver required

Terrain

Delta Base spans 15 acres of mixed woodland with creek crossings, bunker systems, and fortified objectives. The site offers a blend of natural cover and man-made structures, creating varied engagement zones suited to both long-range and close-quarters play.
